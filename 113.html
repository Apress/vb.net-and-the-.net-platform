Errata

First of all, thanks for purchasing this text. I honestly hope you have found it (and are finding it) enjoyable and informative. As many of you may know, this book is a re-working of my first Apress title C# and the .NET Platform. Given that the C# book has already gone through various Errata postings, it was very pleasing to find few errors in the VB.NET translation. Nevertheless, I did find a few glitches that slipped into this first printing.

This Errata page lists any goofs I have found in each chapter. If you happen to find additional errors that have escaped my eyes, fell free to drop me a line and let me know! You can reach me at:

atroelsen@Intertech-inc.com

Take care,
Andrew Troelsen
